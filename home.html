About the EduTrip

In the Edutrip, I went to Yogyakarta and Solo as my destination. There, we visited museums, learned more culture's that are in Indonesia and we also learned many stuffs, including on how to make batik, perfume, tempe, etc.

The photo above this paragraph, is a photo of Candi Prambanan, an example of the destination I visited. It is one of my favorite destination, as it is easy to remember, not to mention the legend that is associated with this Candi, which is named "Roro Jonggrang"
